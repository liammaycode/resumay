  • Democracy Token

    Senior Design Project - completed 2021

    The Democracy Token project was team effort by myself and four other University of Central Florida seniors to create a transparent and accurate election platform. We were able to ensure that only those who were allowed to vote in an election could vote, everyone had only one vote, and real-time election results were viewable to anyone with access to a web browser. We did this by storing election information on the Ethereum blockchain and creating unique and disposable wallet ids for election participants. I was lucky to have such talented team members whom I could develop my skills alongside of.
